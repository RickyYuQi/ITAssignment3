Of the group members, I found that Michael was very good at coordinating the group and acting a chair for our meetings, Domenic and Collin had very good knowledge of the logistics of the project idea, Tomoki and Felix were very good at cracking down on lots of work at a time, and Ricky was very well organised, and consistently ahead of the group. I learnt that for group projects, communication is paramount, as we tended to struggle more when not communicating what we had completed.

I don’t believe that the GitHub logs accurately represent our group activity, as many group members, focused purely on the writing rather than coding. The log also shows that work was mostly completed late in the project, when the truth is that the website was completed later than the rest.
